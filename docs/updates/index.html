Weekly Updates

Autumn Week 9:

During Autumn week 9 we decided a team name and started designing PirateDucks and we though of came up with some questions to ask during our customer meeting. Alice started with a player design, and Marc started with the website

Autumn Week 10:

During Autumn week 10 Alice completed the introduction to the requirements. Charlie formatted and improved the requirements table. Dan Created a UML diagram to display the project stages and started with risk assessment. James dove into the Java game libraries and narrowed it down to LibGdx and Liteengine. Marc continued with the website development and did some fixes.

Later on in the week in another team meeting we confirmed the game library as a group and decided LibGdx. We also decided the editor and went with Intellij IDEA. We also started to discuss the College combat and map design.
